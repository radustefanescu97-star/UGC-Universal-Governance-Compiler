# Reposcan and Bootstrap SOP

A reposcan is incomplete until the agent identifies:
- The **Target Surface** (e.g., `src/`, `config/`, `lib/`).
- The **Protected Neighboring Surfaces** (what should NOT be touched or modified).
- The applicable SOPs and whether the change requires an Approval Gate.

Do not treat a reposcan as just running `ls` or directory listings. It is a deliberate session bootstrap to ensure you know what you are touching and what you must protect.

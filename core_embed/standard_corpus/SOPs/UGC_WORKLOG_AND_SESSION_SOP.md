# Worklog and Session SOP

Every material session working on this project must create or update a worklog in `Plans/worklog.md` (or a designated worklog location).

The worklog must record:
- The Objective
- Target and neighboring surfaces
- Files touched
- Approvals requested or avoided
- Stop reason

Before your execution ends, you MUST append your actions to the main `Plans/worklog.md` file. Do not rely exclusively on internal agent logs like `task.md` or `walkthrough.md` for historical project tracking.
